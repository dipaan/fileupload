The file format is a simple comma separated file with 4 columns. The descriptions of the fields are listed below. Any of the fields may contain an empty value.

1 Date: A date field, with the format of 01/31/2007T23:59:59Z where the 'T' separates the date from the time and the 'Z' indicates the timezone. The T and the Z are always present. The time is a 24-hour time format, with midnight being the 0 hour. The month, day, hour, minute and second fields will all have leading zeros to fill the number to 2 characters.

2 Row Number: A number field, starting at 1 and increments by 1 for each new row.

3 Description: A basic description of the row.

4 Value: The value of the row, expressed as a simple decimal value. The decimal value may or may not have a leading zero (e.g. 0.5 or .5).
